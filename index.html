A Character Guide

Mario is depicted as a portly plumber who lives in the fictional land of the Mushroom Kingdom with Luigi, his younger, taller brother. Mario and Luigi stopped the evil koopa king Bowser from trying to take over the Mushroom Kingdom and marrying princess peach and became heroes.

Princess Peach, the regal ruler of the Mushroom Kingdom in Nintendo's Mario franchise, is known for her iconic pink attire, royal demeanor, and signature parasol. Often finding herself in peril, Peach relies on the heroic efforts of Mario and Luigi. With her kindness and grace, she symbolizes resilience in the face of adversity, making her a central and enduring character in the Nintendo universe.
